Join JSA

Become part of the JSA family today! Membership is open to anyone and everyone. By paying a small donation fee, JSA members will have access to all of our events, as well as member-exclusive perks! If you have any questions, please feel free to look through our FAQ or send us a message here!

Join our Facebook group

Stay updated. We're always on Facebook to keep you updated on our latest events, make announcements, and do cool random stuff. It's our way of keeping in touch with you, so don't miss out on it!

Subscribe to our newsletter

All of our JSA information in one place. We've compiled all of our official announcements, social events, career workshops, and member perks in a single newsletter. Right at your fingertips.

Attend our General Meeting

This is our big welcome to all the new members joining JSA. At the first week of every school year, we put together a giant General Meeting so you can learn about what events we're planning, meet our awesome officers, and get hyped for the year ahead. We will be accepting JSA memberships here as well! Please check our Facebook group for more details!
