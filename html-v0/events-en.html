Events

Want to meet new people, make friends, and create lasting memories? You've come to the right place. We're one of the most active clubs on campus. Here are some of our favorite activities we've done in the past. Check out our Facebook group and our Instagram to see what we're up to now!

Welcome BBQ

Experience JSA tradition with our quarterly barbeque. We organize this event at the beginning of every quarter for both newcomers and returning members to enjoy. It's a great way to meet new people and socialize while we serve you some finger-lickin' barbeque, JSA-style.

Halloween University

Party all Halloween night. Every year, JSAs from five different schools put together a huge clubbing event for you. We've got DJs, we've got dance floors, we've got photobooths. Come dressed in your favorite costume and party with students from other schools! (Photo credits to Masashi Koike)

Ski Trip

Join us on the slopes. We'll be at Big Bear Mountain Resorts for two nights and three days at the beginning of winter quarter, which includes skiing/snowboarding, a cabin party, and unforgettable memories with your JSA family.

Intercollegiate Sports Festival "Undoukai"

Nine schools compete for glory. Represent UCLA and compete against other JSAs with different sports, such as tug-of-war, calvary battle, relay race, and more! We got 1st place for the 2018 "Undoukai"--and we hope you're ready to show your UCLA JSA pride for next year, because we're still hungry for victory!

Hotpot Night

We cook the best homemade Japanese food. We plan many casual food events because...well, we all love Japanese food! We've also done Onigiri festivals, Takoyaki parties, and Sushi nights. Hungry now? Grab a bite with us.
