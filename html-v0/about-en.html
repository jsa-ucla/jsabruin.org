About Us

What is JSA?

Japanese Student Association (JSA) is an official student organization at UCLA founded in 1981, aimed at joining those whom share an interest in Japanese identity, culture, and language. You do not need to know Japanese to join JSA; we are open to students of all backgrounds.

Our mission is to provide students the opportunity to meet new people from differing backgrounds through our social events, to guide students in their career decisions through our professional events, and to create an open atmosphere so that students can have a memorable time at UCLA.
