Career

Our career team serves as the gateway between JSA members and the recruiters from Japanese companies. We connect students with the next step beyond school and provide an opportunity for recruiters to be represented at UCLA, through recruiting events, company infosessions, and career seminars. Join our JSA Career Facebook group for more information. If you have any questions regarding our services, feel free to ask us here!

For Students

Our members can make use of our extensive services to help them guide their professional careers. Here are some of the many services we provide:

  • Company Infosessions
    Our career team invites company representatives from Japan to talk about their company and give advice about how to succeed in their industry.
  • Career Workshops
    We regularly host workshops to give a variety of tips to help you prepare for your professional career through resume reviews, job searching strategies, and interview preparation.
  • On-campus Interviews
    We provide the opportunity to interview with employers from Japanese companies for both full-time jobs and internships. These events are often JSA-exclusive, so don't miss out!

Interested in participating in any of our career events? Join our official UCLA JSA Career Facebook Page. (Please note that most of our events may require Japanese fluency to participate.)

For Recruiters

UCLA JSA boasts in its diverse talent pool; many of our JSA alumni have successful careers in diverse fields, such as banking, engineering, consulting, and marketing. By partnering with us, our career team can offer a variety of services, such as:

  • Hosting on-campus and off-campus events
  • Promotion on our official website, Facebook page, and our newsletter
  • Schedule on-campus interviews with our members

If your company is interested in any of the above, please contact us at uclajsa-career@hotmail.com or fill out the form here. Please include the following information:

  • Name of company
  • Name of representative(s)
  • Contact information
  • Projected date(s)
  • Type of event (company infosession, on-campus interview, etc.)
  • Projected number of participants (students)

We will return your inquiry within the next 3-5 business days.
