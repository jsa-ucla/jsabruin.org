About Us

JSA, NSU, and JPAM are student organizations that collective represent Japanese culture and identity at UCLA. Our mission statements vary slightly to cater to different aspects of our complex community. Our mission is to provide a social/professional community for all students who are interested in Japanese culture and identity.

Visit NSU and JPAM for more information!

Yes! We've also done joint events with NSU/JPAM in the past, so you can experience the best of both worlds.

Our officer application opens at the beginning of winter quarter. The information will be announced on our Facebook group and on our newsletter. Applicants will be given a 10-week intern period before they officially become officers. Feel free to send us a message if you're interested in what we do!
